# Data Gaps & Stakeholder Questions

This document records data gaps, ambiguities, and assumptions identified during data profiling.
Its purpose is to:
- Highlight missing or incomplete information that may affect reporting.
- Capture questions requiring stakeholder clarification.
- Record proposed handling before the semantic model is finalized.

> **Scope**
>
> This document focuses on business-related data gaps and assumptions.
> Data quality issues (e.g. typos, invalid data types, formatting inconsistencies) are documented separately in `data-quality.md`.

| ID | Table | Column | Observation | Evidence | Business Impact | Question for Stakeholder | Proposed Resolution |
|----|-------|--------|-------------|----------|-----------------|--------------------------|---------------------|
| G01 | Date | Date | The Date dimension does not cover the full Sales transaction period. | Date table starts on 2016-01-01; Sales contains transactions from 2015-01-01. | Time Intelligence calculations for 2015 may be incomplete or inaccurate. | — | Extend the Date dimension to include the complete transaction history. |
| G02 | BudgetForecast | Category-Segment | No Budget exists for the **Rural–Productivity** product group. | No corresponding Budget record exists after unpivoting the source file. | Budget KPIs cannot be calculated for this product group. | Is this intentionally excluded, or is Budget data missing? | Leave Budget as NULL and exclude from Budget comparisons until confirmed. |
| G03 | Product | Unit Price, Unit Cost | Only one Unit Price and Unit Cost exist for each product across all years. | No historical pricing table is provided. | Historical Revenue and Gross Profit assume prices remained constant over time. | Should prices be treated as constant throughout the reporting period? | Use the available values and document the assumption. |
| G04 | Sales | Order ID | Sales records do not contain an Order ID. | No OrderID field exists in the Sales dataset. | Order-level KPIs (e.g. Orders, Average Order Value) cannot be calculated reliably. | Does each Sales row represent an Order or an Order Line? | Treat each Sales row as one sales line until confirmed otherwise. |
| G05 | Customer | Customer | Customer information is limited to ID, ZIP Code and Email Name. | No registration date, customer segment or demographic attributes are available. | Customer segmentation and lifecycle analysis cannot be performed. | Is a Customer Master dataset available? | Limit customer reporting to transaction-based metrics. |
| G06 | Campaign | Marketing Cost | Marketing attribution is available, but campaign cost data is not. | No spend, impressions or clicks are included in the dataset. | Marketing efficiency metrics such as ROI, ROAS, CAC and CPA cannot be calculated. | Is marketing spend data available from another source? | Limit reporting to revenue attribution by marketing channel. |
| G07 | Sales / Customer | CustomerID, OrderDate | There is no field indicating whether a transaction is a customer's first purchase or a repeat purchase. | Sales contains only CustomerID and OrderDate; no `IsNewCustomer` or `FirstPurchaseDate` field is provided. | "Khách mới vs quay lại" (Trang 3, biểu đồ ①) and "% mua lại" cannot be calculated without a clear definition of "new." The first period of available data will overstate new customers, since purchase history before the dataset start cannot be checked (left-censoring). | Should "new customer" be defined as first purchase within the dataset's own period, or based on a rolling lookback window (e.g. no purchase in the trailing 12 months)? How should the first available month/year be treated, given it will always look 100% "new"? | Define New Customer = MIN(OrderDate) per CustomerID within the dataset; flag the first period with a footnote due to left-censoring bias rather than excluding it. |
| G08 | Sales | Currency | The reporting currency is not explicitly provided. | No Currency field exists in the source files. | Financial metrics may be interpreted incorrectly if the assumed currency is wrong. | Should all financial values be treated as USD? | Confirm the reporting currency and document it in the Business Glossary. |
| G09 | BudgetForecast | Budget/Forecast | The dataset includes both a Budget and a Forecast. | There are two types of planning data. | Different KPIs can be developed. | Should performance evaluation KPIs be compared to Budget, Forecast, or both? | Design KPIs based on business requirements. |
| G10 | BudgetForecast / Sales | Year, Month | Actual (Sales) history and Budget/Forecast do not cover the same time range. | Actuals span 2015 → partial 2020; Budget/Forecast periods only partially overlap with Actuals. | "% Đạt KH" (Trang 1, biểu đồ ④) and Budget Attainment can only be computed for the overlapping window — computing it outside that window would compare Actual to a period with no matching Budget, or vice versa. | Over which period(s) should Budget Attainment be evaluated — the overlapping window only, or should Forecast be used to fill periods without Budget? | Restrict all Actual-vs-Budget visuals to the overlapping period only; show Forecast-only periods as a separate reference chart, never blended into the same series as Actual vs Budget. |
| G11 | Geo | Region, (sub-region levels) | It is not confirmed whether the Geo dimension has enough hierarchy levels to support drill-down. | Trang 2 requires a "Cấp" (level) column in a matrix designed for drill-down below Region. | If Geo only contains a flat Region field, the drill-down matrix on Trang 2 cannot be built as specified. | Does the Geo table include sub-region levels (e.g. District/State/City) below Region? | Confirm available Geo levels; if only Region exists, reduce the Trang 2 matrix to Region-level only and flag drill-down as not buildable until additional geography data is provided. |
| G12 | Sales | (no Order ID) | "Tần suất mua hàng TB" and "Số đơn theo TrafficChannel" (Trang 3) both require a definition of what counts as one purchase/order. | Same root cause as G04 — no Order ID exists; each Sales row is a line, not a confirmed order. | Without a defined order grain, purchase frequency and order counts will be inflated if computed as "number of Sales rows" instead of "number of distinct orders." | Same as G04 — is each Sales row an order or an order line? Is an Order ID available from another source for Trang 3's order-count metrics? | Until confirmed, define "one purchase" as one distinct (CustomerID, OrderDate) pair rather than one Sales row, and document this as an approximation. |
| G13 | Campaign / Sales | TrafficChannel, Device | It is not confirmed that TrafficChannel and Device exist as usable, populated fields at the Sales grain. | Trang 3 requires both fields for one chart (②) and two filters (TrafficChannel, Device). | If these fields are only populated for campaign-attributed sales (not all transactions), the Trang 3 channel/device chart and filters will silently exclude or misclassify non-attributed sales. | Are TrafficChannel and Device available per transaction, or only at the Campaign level (i.e. blank when a sale has no campaign attribution)? | Confirm field availability and grain; if only available for campaign-attributed sales, document that non-attributed sales will show as "Unknown/Direct." |
